## Description (The Elevated Pitch)

### Option 1 (The Sanctuary)
Sift is a digital sanctuary for your curiosities. Using advanced AI, it transforms chaotic social media links into a structured, editorial-grade library of recipes, routines, and research. Curate your intellectual diet with the quietest tool in your stack.

### Option 2 (The Refiner)
Sift elevates your digital consumption by turning fleeting social media moments into a permanent, curated library. Our intelligent AI extracts the essence of recipes, tutorials, and inspiration, stripping away the noise to leave only what matters. Build a personal archive that respects your attention and refines your taste.

### Option 3 (The Architect)
Turn your passive browsing into an active knowledge base. Sift distills complex content from across the web into structured, beautiful summaries, giving you ownership over the information you consume. Create a sanctuary for your curiosities where clarity replaces clutter.
