# Privacy Policy for Sift

**Last updated: January 13, 2026**

Details matter. Your privacy matters more.

## 1. Introduction
Sift ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and shield your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect minimal data to provide the Sift service:
- **Clipboard Content**: We only access your clipboard when you explicitly open the App to detect URLs you want to save. We do not store or transmit clipboard data unless it is a valid URL you choose to "Sift".
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To curate and save the content you submit.
- To generate AI summaries of the content you save.
- To improve our algorithms and user experience.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party services to power Sift:
- **Supabase**: For secure data storage and authentication.
- **OpenAI**: For content summarization and analysis.
- **Apify**: For content extraction.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hkjstudio.com.
